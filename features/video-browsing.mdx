EmbyTok provides an immersive, TikTok-style browsing experience for your Emby media library. Navigate through your videos with intuitive swipe gestures and discover content in different ways.

## Feed Modes

EmbyTok offers three ways to browse your video content:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Latest">
    Browse videos chronologically from newest to oldest. This feed automatically loads more videos as you scroll, providing an endless browsing experience through your entire library.

    **Best for:** Catching up on recently added content
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Random">
    Discover videos in randomized order. Perfect for serendipitous viewing when you're not sure what to watch. When you reach the end, tap "Refresh" to generate a new randomized playlist.

    **Best for:** Exploring your library and finding forgotten favorites
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Favorites">
    View only videos you've marked as favorites. Your personal curated collection, always at your fingertips.

    **Best for:** Quick access to your most-loved content
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Switching Feed Modes

**On Desktop/Tablet:**
- Look for the feed mode buttons at the top of the screen
- Click between "Latest" (最新), "Random" (随机), and "Favorites" (收藏)

**On Mobile:**
- Tap the menu icon (three lines) in the top left
- Select your desired feed mode from the drawer

## Swipe Navigation

Navigate through videos with natural vertical gestures:

- **Swipe Up:** Go to the next video
- **Swipe Down:** Return to the previous video
- **Tap:** Pause/play the current video

Videos automatically start playing as they come into view, creating a seamless browsing experience.

## Orientation Filtering

Control which video orientations appear in your feed to match your viewing preference and device.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Vertical Only" icon="mobile">
    Show only portrait/vertical videos (9:16, etc.)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Horizontal Only" icon="tv">
    Show only landscape/widescreen videos (16:9, etc.)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Both" icon="maximize">
    Show all videos regardless of orientation
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Setting Your Orientation Preference

1. Open the menu (three-line icon in top left)
2. Tap "Settings" at the bottom
3. Under "Display Preferences," select your preferred orientation mode
4. The feed will automatically reload with filtered content

<Info>
  **Smart Defaults:** EmbyTok automatically selects "Vertical Only" on mobile devices and "Both" on tablets/desktops to match typical viewing patterns.
</Info>

## View Modes

Switch between two different viewing experiences:

### Feed View (Default)

Full-screen, immersive TikTok-style browsing. Videos fill the entire screen with vertical scrolling navigation.

### Grid View

See multiple videos at once in a thumbnail grid. Perfect for:
- Quickly scanning through many videos
- Finding a specific video by thumbnail
- Getting an overview of your library

Tap the grid icon in the top right to switch between feed and grid views.

<Note>
  Grid view automatically activates on landscape-oriented devices (like TVs and tablets) for easier navigation.
</Note>

## Auto-Play Mode

Enable continuous playback to watch videos back-to-back without interaction:

1. Tap the infinity (∞) icon on the right side of the screen
2. When a video ends, the next one automatically begins
3. All UI elements fade away for distraction-free viewing

**Auto-Play Features:**
- Seamless transitions between videos
- Pure viewing mode with minimal UI
- Automatic progression through your feed
- Tap the infinity icon again to disable

<Tip>
  Auto-play is perfect for background viewing or when you want to discover new content without manual navigation.
</Tip>
