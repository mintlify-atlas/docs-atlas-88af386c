EmbyTok provides intuitive playback controls designed for touch-first interaction. Control your viewing experience with gestures, on-screen controls, and keyboard shortcuts.

## Basic Playback

### Play/Pause

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Touch">
    **Tap anywhere** on the video to toggle between play and pause.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Button">
    Use the **play/pause button** in the bottom-left corner of the progress bar.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Keyboard">
    Press **Space** or **Enter** to play/pause.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Videos automatically begin playing when they scroll into view and pause when you navigate away.

## Audio Controls

### Mute/Unmute

**Toggle Audio:**
- Tap the **volume icon** in the top-right corner
- Press **M** on keyboard
- Audio state persists across videos

**Volume Indicator:**
- **Red X icon:** Audio is muted
- **White speaker icon:** Audio is playing

**Spinning Disc Indicator:**

The spinning disc at the bottom-right of each video shows playback status:
- Spinning: Video is playing
- Red border: Audio is muted
- Gray border: Audio is enabled

<Info>
  EmbyTok starts with audio muted by default for a better browsing experience. Unmute when you find a video you want to watch with sound.
</Info>

## Video Seeking & Progress

### Progress Bar

A seek bar appears at the bottom of each video showing:
- Current playback position
- Total video duration
- Visual timeline of progress

**To seek:**
1. Tap and drag the progress slider left/right
2. Video playback jumps to your selected position
3. Release to continue playback

### Swipe to Seek

Use horizontal swipe gestures for quick seeking:

1. **Swipe right** on the video → Fast forward
2. **Swipe left** on the video → Rewind
3. A preview overlay shows your seek offset (e.g., "+15s" or "-10s")
4. Release to apply the seek

<Tip>
  The further you swipe, the larger the seek offset. Each 5 pixels of swipe equals approximately 1 second of seek time.
</Tip>

### Keyboard Seeking

- **Right Arrow:** Skip forward 10 seconds
- **Left Arrow:** Skip backward 10 seconds

## Speed Controls

### 2x Playback Speed

Hold your finger down on the video for **long press** (500ms+) to activate 2x playback speed:

1. Touch and hold anywhere on the video
2. After 0.5 seconds, a "2x Speed" indicator appears
3. Video plays at double speed while you hold
4. Release to return to normal speed

<Note>
  This is perfect for quickly scrubbing through videos to find interesting moments without manually seeking.
</Note>

## Fullscreen Mode

### Entering Fullscreen

**Methods:**
- Tap the **fullscreen icon** (maximize) in the top-right corner
- On supported devices, fullscreen gives you immersive viewing

**Exit Fullscreen:**
- Tap the **minimize icon** (replaces maximize when in fullscreen)
- Press **Esc** on keyboard
- Use your browser's fullscreen exit gesture

<Accordion title="Device-Specific Fullscreen Behavior">
  **Mobile Devices:**
  - Fullscreen hides browser UI completely
  - May require screen rotation for best experience
  - Some mobile browsers handle fullscreen differently
  
  **Desktop:**
  - Standard browser fullscreen (F11-like)
  - All browser chrome hidden
  - Press Esc to exit
  
  **Tablets:**
  - Works like mobile in portrait mode
  - Like desktop in landscape mode
</Accordion>

## Video Information

### Viewing Details

Each video displays information at the bottom of the screen:

- **Video title**
- **Production year** (if available)
- **Duration** in minutes
- **Media type** badge
- **Description/Overview** (tap "Info" to expand)

**Expand Full Description:**
1. Tap the **Info icon** (ℹ️) on the right sidebar
2. The description area expands to show full details
3. Tap again to collapse

<Info>
  You can also tap directly on the truncated description text to expand it without using the Info button.
</Info>

## Video Rotation

For videos with incorrect orientation, use the rotation control:

1. Tap the **rotation icon** (↻) on the right sidebar
2. Video rotates 90° clockwise
3. Tap again to continue rotating (0°, 90°, 180°, 270°)
4. Rotation resets when you navigate to a different video

This is useful for:
- Phone-recorded videos in wrong orientation
- Sideways content
- Adjusting vertical videos on horizontal screens

## Touch Gesture Summary

| Gesture | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Single Tap** | Play/Pause |
| **Swipe Up** | Next video |
| **Swipe Down** | Previous video |
| **Swipe Left/Right** | Seek backward/forward |
| **Long Press** | 2x playback speed |
| **Drag Progress Bar** | Manual seek |

<Warning>
  Some gestures (like swipe to seek) won't trigger if you swipe too vertically. Keep your swipe mostly horizontal for seeking, or mostly vertical for video navigation.
</Warning>
